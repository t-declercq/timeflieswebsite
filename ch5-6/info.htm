About Us

Joe Fly

Founder/CEO of Time Flies Fly Fishing

Joe Fly created Time Flies Fly Fishing in 1986 during a binge-eating session on the Great Lake of Detroit. All he had was a computer, a weak cellular connection, and a bunch of flies! Joe had one loyal customer for 15 years, and no others until 2001 when the .com boom allowed his genius to reach across the world. Joe credits his success to his remarkable knowledge of fly fishing. Joe Fly is often seen looking at himself in the mirror in his bedroom where he spends most of his time. Joe is currently single, and plans to stay that way. He also credits his success to his life-size cardboard cutout of himself in the corner of his room. "He- - I motivate me so much. I shouldn't be allowed 6ft near myself, or else I might takeover the world!"
